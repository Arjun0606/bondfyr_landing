Cookie Policy

Last Updated: March 15, 2025

This Cookie Policy explains how Bondfyr ("we", "us", or "our") uses cookies and similar technologies on our website and mobile application (collectively, the "Service"). By using our Service, you consent to the use of cookies as described in this policy.

1. What Are Cookies?

Cookies are small text files that are stored on your device when you visit a website or use a mobile application. They are widely used to make websites and applications work more efficiently and provide valuable information to the owners of the site or app.

2. Types of Cookies We Use

We use the following types of cookies:

  • Essential Cookies: These cookies are necessary for the Service to function properly. They enable core functionality such as security, network management, and account access. You cannot opt out of these cookies.
  • Functionality Cookies: These cookies allow us to remember choices you make and provide enhanced, personalized features. For example, they may remember your login details or language preferences.
  • Analytical/Performance Cookies: These cookies collect information about how you use our Service, which helps us improve its performance. They allow us to count visits and traffic sources, and to see how visitors move around our Service.
  • Targeting/Advertising Cookies: These cookies record your visit to our Service, the pages you have visited, and the links you have followed. We use this information to make our Service and the advertising displayed more relevant to your interests.
  • Social Media Cookies: These cookies are set by social media services that we have added to the Service to enable you to share content with your friends and networks.

3. Third-Party Cookies

In addition to our own cookies, we may also use various third-party cookies to report usage statistics, deliver advertisements, and so on. These cookies may include:

  • Analytics Providers: We use Google Analytics to help us understand how our Service is being used. These cookies collect information about your use of our Service, including your IP address, browser type, referring/exit pages, and date/time stamps.
  • Advertising Networks: We work with third-party advertising networks that collect information about your online activities to provide you with relevant advertisements on other websites and applications.
  • Social Media Platforms: We allow you to share content on social media platforms like Facebook, Twitter, and Instagram. These platforms may set cookies when you use these features.

4. Mobile Device Identifiers

For our mobile application, we may use mobile device identifiers (such as Apple's IDFA or Android's Advertising ID) instead of or in addition to cookies. These identifiers work similarly to cookies but are specific to your mobile device.

5. How to Manage Cookies

Most web browsers allow you to control cookies through their settings. You can typically find these settings in the "Options" or "Preferences" menu of your browser. You can also use the following links for more information on how to manage cookies in common browsers:

  • Google Chrome
  • Mozilla Firefox
  • Safari
  • Microsoft Edge

For mobile devices, you can adjust your privacy settings to limit ad tracking:

  • iOS devices: Go to Settings > Privacy > Advertising > Toggle "Limit Ad Tracking" to on
  • Android devices: Go to Settings > Google > Ads > Toggle "Opt out of Ads Personalization" to on

Please note that if you choose to block or delete cookies, you may not be able to access certain features of our Service, and your user experience may be affected.

6. Do Not Track Signals

Some browsers have a "Do Not Track" feature that signals to websites that you visit that you do not want to have your online activity tracked. Our Service does not currently respond to "Do Not Track" signals.

7. Cookie Consent

When you first visit our Service, we may ask for your consent to place cookies on your device. You can change your cookie preferences at any time by clicking on the "Cookie Settings" link in the footer of our website or in the settings menu of our mobile application.

8. Changes to This Cookie Policy

We may update our Cookie Policy from time to time. We will notify you of any changes by posting the new Cookie Policy on this page and updating the "Last Updated" date. You are advised to review this Cookie Policy periodically for any changes.
